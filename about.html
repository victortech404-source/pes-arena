The PES ARENA Story

From campus gaming to national competitive eFootball infrastructure.

Our Mission

PES ARENA was established in 2025 as a national competitive eFootball infrastructure designed to bridge the gap between campus gaming and professional esports. What began as a university-based initiative has evolved into a comprehensive platform serving gamers nationwide.

Our vision is to create a unified ecosystem where eFootball enthusiasts can compete, connect, and advance their skills in a structured, competitive environment. We're building the foundation for Kenya's eFootball future by providing professional-grade tools and tournaments accessible to all.

National Reach, Local Impact

PES ARENA is open to all universities, colleges, and independent regional gaming communities across Kenya. We believe in inclusive competition that brings together the best talent from every corner of the country, creating pathways from amateur to professional eFootball.
